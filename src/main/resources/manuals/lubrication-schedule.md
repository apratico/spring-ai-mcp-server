# Plant-wide Lubrication Schedule

Cross-reference for shift supervisors and maintenance technicians. Frequency is in operating hours unless stated otherwise.

| Asset                | Point                       | Lubricant            | Quantity          | Interval (hrs) |
|----------------------|-----------------------------|----------------------|-------------------|----------------|
| CNC-07, CNC-08       | Linear guides (X, Y, Z)     | NLGI 2 lithium       | 2 strokes / fitting| 50             |
| CNC-07               | A and C rotary axis bearings| NLGI 2 high-speed    | 1 stroke / fitting | 100            |
| PRESS-02, PRESS-03   | Tie-rod nut threads          | Anti-seize copper    | thin film          | 500            |
| PRESS-03             | Pitman bearings              | EP-2 lithium         | 2 strokes / fitting| 250            |
| LINE-A, LINE-B       | Drive-pulley bearings        | NLGI 2 lithium       | 1 stroke / fitting | 500            |
| All assets           | Hydraulic oil sample         | ISO VG 46            | 100 ml sample      | 1000           |

## Notes

- Over-greasing pulley bearings is a leading cause of grease-purge into the conveyor belt and an indirect contributor to belt slip (alarm **E-124**).
- Hydraulic oil sampling must be taken from the dedicated sampling port, not from the reservoir top — otherwise particle counts are biased high by stratified contamination.
- Switching between lithium and calcium-sulfonate greases at the same point requires full purge of the old grease through the relief fitting.
